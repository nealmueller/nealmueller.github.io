Phonetic Alfa

Learn and use NATO and LAPD phonetic alphabets with drill cards, quizzes, and fast speak-back for call-ready code readback.

View on the App Store Privacy Policy

What It Does

  • Convert typed text into clear phonetic readback instantly.
  • Practice with swipeable drill cards and short quiz rounds.
  • Switch between NATO and LAPD alphabets inside the app.
